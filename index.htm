ㅤㅤㅤHTML and webpage programming have been a fascinating topic for me since Grade 8, and so far ICT 9 never failed to live up to that level. Amongst the deep dive into the complexities of website programming, our ICT teacher Ms. Uminga was able to teach all the concepts needed (even if she did have to catch us up on the stuff we missed last grade), and enabled us to fully understand and apply these in our website coding journey. From learning the etiquettes of XHTML to making lists, tables, hyperlinks and even our own working forms, this quarter has improved my knowledge on HTML and has allowed me to create all sorts of websites with those in mind. While I think I did a pretty good job this quarter, there's always room for improvement, and for me that includes putting a bit more effort into studying our lessons so that I can be more prepared for quizzes and tests.

Verdict: ★★★★✰
